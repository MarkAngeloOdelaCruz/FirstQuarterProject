What Is This Website About?

This webpage is to propose a solution related to the SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities. This SDG aims to improve the sustainability of our cities and communities. It also promotes sound urban planning, investing in renewable energy, and protecting cities from environmental threats. This webpage is created to spread more awareness about this SDG and the issues surrounding it, and to push people to work towards a solution to prevent these issues from worsening. Hopefully, through this webpage, people can learn more about this SDG and its importance in our lives.

  • Introduce SDG 11 and its importance
  • Raise awareness about issues surrounding the SDG
  • Propose a solution to prevent these issues
  • Educate people and push them to spread this information
